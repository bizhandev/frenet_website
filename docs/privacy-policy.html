  • Limited profile information needed to operate the connection (e.g., account name).

From you (directly):

  • Contact details (name, email) for admin use.

  • Configuration settings (e.g., time zones, report presets).

We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of personal data.

3) How we use the data

  • To authenticate with Pinterest and fetch organic and paid analytics you request.

Legal bases (EEA/UK): our legitimate interests in providing analytics tooling; performance of a contract where applicable; and/or your consent where required.
