Privacy Policy for Frenet

Effective date: 01 September 2025

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard information when you use Frenet mobile app, including our integrations with the Pinterest API. This document is intended for business users of the app. It is not legal advice.

1) What this policy covers

  • Our processing of data obtained via Pinterest API for organic and paid analytics (Pins, campaigns, ad accounts, and related performance metrics).
  • Basic account information you provide to set up and access the app.

We do not sell personal data.

2) Data we collect

From Pinterest (via API, with your authorization):

  • Account and object identifiers (e.g., user/account IDs, board IDs, pin IDs, ad account/campaign/ad group/ad IDs).
  • Analytics/metrics and reporting dimensions (e.g., impressions, clicks, CTR, saves, conversions, spend, revenue/ROAS where available, dates, granularity).
  • Limited profile information needed to operate the connection (e.g., account name).

From you (directly):

  • Contact details (name, email) for admin use.
  • OAuth tokens/refresh tokens required to access the Pinterest API on your behalf.
  • Configuration settings (e.g., time zones, report presets).

We do not intentionally collect sensitive categories of personal data.

3) How we use the data

  • To authenticate with Pinterest and fetch organic and paid analytics you request.
  • To generate dashboards and reports for your internal use.
  • To provide support, troubleshooting, usage analytics (non-identifying), and to improve the app.
  • To comply with Pinterest Developer policies and applicable law.

Legal bases (EEA/UK): legitimate interests in providing analytics tooling; performance of a contract where applicable; and/or your consent where required.

4) Data sharing & disclosure

We do not share your data with third parties except:

  • Service providers (sub-processors) under contract (e.g., cloud hosting, monitoring, error logging) — access only to perform services and with appropriate protections.
  • Legal requirements (e.g., subpoena) or to protect rights, safety, or prevent abuse.
  • Pinterest — data flows to/from Pinterest strictly via API under your authorization; we follow Pinterest Terms and Platform Policies.

A current list of sub-processors is available on request: info@frenet-company.com.

5) Data storage, location & retention

  • Data and tokens are stored in secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Access is limited to authorized personnel under least-privilege controls.
  • OAuth tokens are encrypted at rest; all network traffic uses TLS.
  • We keep report data for 6 months unless you request earlier deletion or a different retention period is agreed.

6) Your choices & rights

  • Revoke access: You may revoke Frenet mobile app Pinterest access at any time in your Pinterest settings or by contacting us; we will cease API access and invalidate tokens.
  • Delete data: Email info@frenet-company.com to request deletion of stored tokens and report data. We will confirm completion within a reasonable period.
  • Access/Rectification: You may request a copy or correction of your data we hold.

EEA/UK users: you may have rights to restrict processing, portability, and to object; you can lodge a complaint with your local supervisory authority.
California residents: we do not “sell” personal information as defined by the CCPA/CPRA. You may request access or deletion as above.

7) Children’s privacy

Our services are intended for business use and are not directed to children under 13.

8) Security

We implement administrative, technical, and physical safeguards appropriate to the nature of the data. No method is 100% secure; we strive to continuously improve.

9) International transfers

If we transfer data across borders, we use appropriate safeguards (e.g., standard contractual clauses or other lawful mechanisms). Details on request.

10) Changes to this policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. We will post the new version with a new “Effective date” and, where required, notify you of material changes.

11) Contact us

Questions or requests: info@frenet-company.com.
